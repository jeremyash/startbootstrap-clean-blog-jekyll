I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Botany at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am currently researching how the abundance and distribution of understory plant species have changed over the past 50 years, and to what degree has climate change been a driving factor. I use a variety of statistical and modeling tools, and several large datasets to understand how species respond to environmental change over time and space.
